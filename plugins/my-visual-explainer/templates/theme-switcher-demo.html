      Collapsible Section

      Implementation Details (click to expand)

      The theme engine uses a closure pattern (ThemeSwitcher IIFE) with three public methods: init(), setPreset(index), and toggleMode().

      Mermaid re-rendering works by reading the original source from <script type="text/plain" class="diagram-source"> elements, calling mermaid.render() with updated themeVariables, and replacing the SVG in the canvas.

      How to test

      Click the dropdown in the top-right corner to switch between all 8 presets. Click the moon/sun button to toggle light/dark mode. Watch fonts, colors, badges, cards, table, diff panels, and the Mermaid diagram all update in sync.
